WHO WE ARE

The Circle of Lift Foundation consist of a group of diverse, highly skilled and trained professional women, who have come together to create a bridge between corporations looking for diverse talent and individuals whose talents are under-recognised.

2


WHAT WE DO

We work closely with organisations ranging from Government to Fortune 500 companies to Tech start-ups, to develop high-impact partnerships around inclusion and talent access, so that our female professionals and leaders are given the opportunities to reach their full potential.

3


OUR PRINCIPLES

The principles of respect, transparency, trust and integrity are fundamental to the core of our business. These are the basis on which we conduct our business and the same basis will be applied to our partners. We adhere to strict corporate governance and King IV principles.

WHY US

We understand the value and social impact that can be achieved, by  empowering female professionals and  entrepreneurs across rural and urban  areas across African countries. 

We aim to provide opportunities,  resources, investment and access to  our network, so that these women can  operate at senior levels or as owner  managed businesses. Ensuring we  build sustainable businesses with  improved shareholder value. 

Our focus will be on six key strategic  industry sectors, that we believe will  benefit from our investment.

FinTech

Manufacturing

Hospitality

Technology

Education

Healthcare

STRATEGIC OBJECTIVES
